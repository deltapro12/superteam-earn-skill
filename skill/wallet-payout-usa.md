# Wallet payout — United States

USDC/USDG from Superteam Earn → USD in your US bank account.

## Receive bounty payout

1. Connect **Phantom** to Superteam Earn at signup
2. Winnings arrive as **USDC**, **USDG**, or **SOL** on Solana
3. Verify on https://solscan.io

## Convert to bank USD

```
Phantom (USDC) → Coinbase or Kraken (Solana SPL) → ACH to US bank
```

### Coinbase (recommended)

1. Coinbase account + identity verification (SSN for US residents)
2. Phantom → Send USDC → Coinbase deposit address (**Solana network only**)
3. Sell USDC → USD on Coinbase
4. Withdraw USD via **ACH** to linked bank (typically 1–3 business days, often same-day)

### Kraken (alternative)

1. Kraken account + verification
2. Deposit USDC on **Solana** network
3. Convert to USD → withdraw via ACH/wire

### Phantom debit card (optional)

Phantom offers a debit card in supported US states — spend USDC directly without exchange step. Check availability at phantom.com.

## Fees (typical)

| Step | Cost |
|------|------|
| Solana transfer | ~$0.01 |
| Coinbase sell spread | ~0.5–1.5% |
| ACH withdrawal | Usually free |
| **$400 USDG net** | ~$394–398 in bank |

## No-capital signup

- Phantom wallet: **free**, empty at registration
- Coinbase/Kraken: **free** to create; no deposit required
- Capital only needed for buy-in bounties (not dev/skills work)

## Tax note (US)

Superteam payouts in USDG/USDC may be taxable income. Track amounts received; consult a tax professional. Coinbase/Kraken provide transaction history for reporting.

## Safety

- Never share seed phrase
- Always use **Solana** network for USDC between Phantom ↔ exchange
- Enable 2FA on exchange accounts
